open source intelligence resources:

CIA archives
– unorganised – random institutional repository – literally every declassified document ever mentioning the CIA
https://www.cia.gov/readingroom/

Office of the Historian
– owned by US government, curated documents
https://history.state.gov/

Internet Archive
– everything??
https://archive.org/

search Wikileaks
– Cablegate is maybe illegal
https://search.wikileaks.org/plusd/

National Security Archive
– academic non-profit – sues US government to get them to give up secret documents and make the information available in books and online
https://nsarchive.gwu.edu/

Hathi Trust
– older documents, articles, and books – search in quotes
https://www.hathitrust.org/

Wilson Centre's digital archive
– history conference, specialise in translated government documents from the Cold War – academic
https://digitalarchive.wilsoncenter.org/

Libgen
- Internet Archive alternative for books and articles - RIP internet archive
https://www.libgen.is/

Z Library
- Internet Archive alternative for books and articles - RIP internet archive
