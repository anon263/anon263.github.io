anonymous publishing house

-- welcome to the anonymous publishing house --

ah the allure of academia ... submitting your most precious works to be scrutinized and inevitably rejected by a soulless corporate machine. or maybe you're one of the lucky ones, you scrape past the suits with agendas poised to kill your darlings -- never unscathed; now you're published in some journal no one reads. your beloved is safely tucked away somewhere in a rubber stamped abyss. screaming into the entropic void that defines the post-modern academy. relegated to the tomes of 'literature', the dustbin of history. through the anonymous publishing house we attempt to enable ourselves, and you, us -- the unwashed masses -- to scream into a void of our own making.
